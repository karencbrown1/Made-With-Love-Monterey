About Made With Love Monterey

Every piece starts with a block, a carving tool, and a deep love for this stretch of California coast.

I hand-carve each design into a linoleum block — sculpting every wave, every fin, every arch of a whale's breach — then roll it with ink and press it by hand onto quality tees and sweatshirts. It's an old craft, and an intentional one. No digital files, no screens, no machines. Just me, my tools, and the images that inspire me most: the sea life of Monterey Bay and the rugged coastal landmarks we're lucky enough to call home.

From humpback whales rising through churning swells to the iconic curves of Bixby Bridge, each design is a little piece of the Central Coast you can actually wear. And because every print is hand-pressed, no two are exactly alike — which is kind of the whole point.

Have something special in mind? I love taking on custom orders. Whether it's a favorite sea creature, a meaningful local landmark, or something else close to your heart, I'm happy to carve a new design just for you. Reach out and let's make something together.
